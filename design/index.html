Design



Breckland Utility Solutions Limited is a GIRS-registered gas design company and can support your gas connections business by offering the following design services for networks up to 2 bar pressure:

  • Outline designs for the purpose of costing and tendering.
  • Detailed designs for construction.
  • Design administration, including arranging the CSEP agreement and gaining design validation from the adopting Gas Transporter.
  • Independent audit.

We work closely with the UIP, adopting utility company and other stakeholders to achieve the best outcomes for all. Because we understand construction, our designs minimise the UIP’s exposure to safety, technical and commercial risk, helping achieve a smooth passage through the construction phase.

Using the latest GASWorkS 10 software, we pride ourselves in creating clear and accurate designs from project inception through to the hand-over brief. We take care of all the technical administration, providing the UIP with a fully approved and validated design leaving the UIP free to concentrate on delivering the physical work.
