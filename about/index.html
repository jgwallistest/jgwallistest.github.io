About

Established in 2018

Breckland Utility Solutions Limited was established by Bob and Tricia Beavis in May 2018 to support the design, construction, and management of gas supply networks. Bob and Tricia are both highly experienced, having worked in the gas industry for many years, and Bob is a Chartered Gas Engineer.
