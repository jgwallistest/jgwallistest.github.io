Terms

Bookings

Bookings may be made up to eighteen months in advance of the beginning of your trip. You can book either by e-mail or telephone (for short-term arrangements, it is probably best to contact us by telephone).

Confirmation of your booking will depend on the availability of a suitable vehicle for the requested trip. We will always try hard to arrange a vehicle and driver for you, but this will usually be easier if you book at least six months in advance of your planned trip.

Payment

All payments are to be made in Indian Rupees (INR) only. Regarding any individual tour:

  • 50% to be paid at or before the beginning of the tour
  • 25% to be paid half way through the tour
  • 25% to be paid at the end of the tour

No commission

Our drivers never get paid for recommending or taking you to particular hotels, restaurants, or places of interest. Some firms are happy for their drivers to accept such payments, but you can be sure that we never take kick-backs.
