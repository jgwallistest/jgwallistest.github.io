Technical Services to the Gas Industry

Consultancy

Utilities work can be a complex business but we aim for the highest standards by keeping things as simple as possible, making it easier to get things right first time. We’ve got a wealth of experience of both large and small utility companies as well as the industry economic regulator, Ofgem, covering a wide range of activities.

High Standards

Achieving high standards is very important to us and we operate a quality management system conforming to the internationally renowned ISO9001 standard. We are also certified for compliance with the ISO 14001 environmental management standard. You can easily verify our ISO registrations.

Support, Advice and Audit

Led by an experienced Chartered Engineer, we’re a GIRS-registered design organisation meaning we can offer a gas network design service on behalf of gas utility infrastructure providers (UIPs) and provide support, advice and audit to other design organisations. You can easily verify our GIRS registration.

SHEA Training

Our chartered engineer also offers SHEA (Safety, Health and Environment Awareness) training for gas, power and water utilities, and can offer other technical training specifically tailored to your needs.

Other Accreditations

As well as our quality management and design registrations, we’re also Gold Members of Constructionline and have Acclaim Accreditation.
