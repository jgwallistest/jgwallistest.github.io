Welcome to the Essex Field Trial Society

The Essex Field Trial Society welcomes you to their new improved user-friendly website.

The society is long-established in the gundog world and prides itself in providing high-quality trials for both Spaniels and Retrievers.

The society, formed in 1950, is lucky to have access to some of the best trialling grounds in the country and it is largely due to this that membership has now exceeded 600.

We are dedicated to the selection of the best winning dogs at our trials, therefore helping to maintain and improve the standard of all participating gundog breeds.

The Essex Field Trial Society is affiliated to The Kennel Club.
