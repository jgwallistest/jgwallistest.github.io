Utility companies

Gas design providers

We know that experienced design staff can be hard to find. If you’re GIRS-registered for gas design we can support you with a design service for either one-off projects or on an ongoing basis.

  • Outline designs for the purpose of costing and tendering.
  • Detailed designs for construction.
  • Technical design review and approval by our chartered engineer.
  • Independent audit.
