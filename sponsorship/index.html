Sponsorship

Connor Rossi Thomson

At Breckland Utility Solutions Limited we enjoy motorcycle racing when we’re not working. We’re proud sponsors of Connor Rossi Thomson of CRT Racing, who has raced successfully both in the UK and abroad, and during 2020 competed in the National Superstock 600 championship at the British Superbike rounds.

Toby Reynolds

Also a friend in the racing paddock is up and coming rider Toby Reynolds, who we supported and looked out for in the same series of races as Connor.
