About the Essex Field Trials Society

The Essex Field Trial Society started in 1950 is now able to run 10 trials a year for Retrievers and Spaniels.

These consist of:

  • a 2-day Open Qualifier Stake for Retrievers
  • a 1-day Open Stake for Cocker Spaniels
  • a 1-day Open Stake for Springer Spaniels
  • and Novice Stakes for Retrievers and Spaniels,
