Field trials

The Essex Field Trial Society is lucky to have access to some of the best trialling grounds in the country and hold Field Trial events for Retrievers and Spaniels. We are dedicated to the selection of the best winning dogs at our trials, therefore helping to maintain and improve the standard of all participating gundog breeds.

The full current year Field Trial Schedules and Events can be downloaded from this page once all details have been finalised along with copies of the Field Trial Entry Forms for each event. Results from all trials will be added to our news page.

Please do not hesitate to contact the relevant Field Trial Secretary in the unlikely event that you encounter any problem in downloading documents.

Sponsorship
