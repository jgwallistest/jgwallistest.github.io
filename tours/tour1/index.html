Tour of Rajasthan (14 days)

Day 1

I will meet you at the airport and drop you off at your hotel.

Day 2

Mandawa - See havelis, some of Northern India’s oldest architecture.

Day 3

Bikaner - See Junagarh Fort, Old Street, Havelis Street, and the old bazaar.

Day 4

Khury - Go on a camel safari, a Jeep safari, then at sunset enjoy a traditional dance programme.

Day 5

Jaisalmer - here you will see Jaisalmer Fort, Bada Bagh, Salim Singh haveli, Nathuram haveli, Pawta haveli, and some impressive streets.

Day 6

Jodhpur - here you will see the Mehrangarh Fort, Jaswant Thada, the clock tower, the bazaar, and a spice market.

Day 7

Ranakpur - here you will see Jain temples.

Days 8 and 9

Udaipur - here you will see City Place, Jagdish Temple, a lake, Queen’s Garden, sunset point, and the Bagore ki Haveli Museum.

Day 10

In Pushkar you will see the world-famous Bharmha Temple lake, Shadus, and the Pilgrim’s Centre.

Days 11 and 12

In Jaipur you will see City Place, Hawa Mahal, the Amber Fort, the Jaigarh Fort, and Nahargarh.

Day 13

I will drive you to Agra to see the world-famous Taj Mahal, the Red Fort, the Baby Taj (etmad-ul-dola), and the Mehtatab Bagh (at the back of the Taj Mahal, where they want to build the black Taj Mahal).

Day 14

I will drive you back to Delhi.
