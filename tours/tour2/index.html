Tour of Delhi, Agra, Ranthembhor, and Jaipur (7 days)

Day 1

Get picked up from the airport, then dropped off straight away at your first hotel, or go sightseeing in Delhi first then get taken to the hotel later.

Day 2

Get picked up from your hotel and driven to Agra. After checking in at a local hotel, see the Red Fort, the Taj Mahal, the Baby Taj, and the Mehtatab Bagh. If you wish, you can see the sunrise at the Taj Mahal. When the day’s sightseeing is over, stay overnight in your local hotel.

Day 3

Get picked up from your hotel and driven to Ranthembhor. On the way you will see Fatehpur Sikri, which was the first capital of Mahal. Stay overnight in Ranthembhor.

Day 4

You can go on a camel safari, or a Jeep safari, or spend the day however you like.

Day 5

Get picked up from your hotel and driven to Jaipur, then check-in at your local hotel. After a rest, you can go sightseeing. Places of interest include the Birla temple and the Pink City. After the sightseeing, you will stay overnight in your hotel.

Day 6

Get picked up from your hotel and taken to see more sights of Jaipur. These will include the City Palace, Hawa Mahal, the Amber Fort, Jaigarh, and Nahargarh. Then you will stay in your local hotel for a second night.

Day 7

Get picked up from your local hotel, driven to Delhi, then dropped off at the airport, your original hotel, or another hotel.
