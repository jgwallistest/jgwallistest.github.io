Prices

Indian currency

In India, the national currency is called the Indian Rupee, which is abbreviated to INR. Obviously the exchange rate varies, but at the time of writing, £1 was worth 91 INR.

Airport transfers

Collection from Delhi airport - 1000 INR (this includes the parking fee)
Drop-off at Delhi airport - 700 INR

Sightseeing in Delhi

Daily rate per car - 1,400 INR (up to 80 km / 8 hours, with parking fees included)

Additional fees are:

  • Extra hours over the included 8 hours: 100 INR per hour
  • Extra distance over the included 80 km: 12 INR per km

Tours outside Delhi

Daily rate per car: 2,800 INR

This daily rate includes:

  • Up to 200 km driving per day (averaged across the whole tour)
  • Fuel
  • Driver allowance (for food and accommodation)
  • Insurance
  • Parking fees
  • Road tolls and taxes
  • Passenger tax

Additional fees are:

  • Extra distance over the included 200 km per day: 12 INR per km
  • State road taxes for taxis driving through specific states: about 200–300 INR per car per day
  • Monument guides
  • Your food and accommodation
  • Your entrance fees
  • Tips, etc.

Distances

All tour distances are calculated from / to the centre of Delhi, which is Connaught Place.
