Consultancy



Utilities work can be a complex business but we aim for the highest standards by keeping things as simple as possible, making it easier to get things right first time. We’ve got a wealth of experience of both large and small utility companies as well as the industry economic regulator, Ofgem, covering a wide range of activities.

We also have a detailed understanding of the Lloyd’s Register Utility Schemes, for example GIRS. If you would like to move towards becoming a registered designer or Utility Infrastructure Provider or you already hold the registration but need further advice and support, Breckland Utility Solutions can help you.

You’re assured of a friendly, personal and confidential service. Please talk to us about how we can help you to support and develop your company’s business.
