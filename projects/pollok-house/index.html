Pollok House

Project Remit

Breckland Utility Solutions Limited was awarded a contract to design a gas network for a brand new supply to Pollok House, which is a stately home in Pollok Country Park near Glasgow. Pollok House was built in 1752 and is administered by the National Trust for Scotland. More information about Pollok House can be found at the National Trust for Scotland website.
